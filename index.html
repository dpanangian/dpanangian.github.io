About

Hi! I'm a CS Student based in Munich with a passion for crunching data. Since the beginning I always put Information amongst the things I value most. Someone said Knowing your history can make you a better fighter in the present. But having a meaningful information doesn't always mean that it's useful. Dissecting data, finding some patterns, trends or anomalies, doing comparations are both exciting and intriguing for me.


Programming is also been a thing. I remember in those early blogging era, i would tweaked my blog with many widgets and having my own edited images to look as cool as possible. These two brought me to my journey in Data Science.


Now outside of university I spend my time mostly working on end-to-end data science projects. That usually begin from collecting data from databases and end with development of an user-friendly app whether in form of a chatbot or classifier.

Take a look at some of the topics i'm interested in:

  • Explainable AI
  • NLP
  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Information Visualization

On top of doing my masters and working part time I like to do small projects on more novel and experimental topics of machine learning. I find that there is always something new to learn in the fast-paced world of AI.

In my free time i also like to do music and graphic design.
